About Me

I originally went to college to become a doctor. I quickly realized the expense and time commitment were greater than my love of medicine. I decided to work at a physician office on the administrative side and found a lot of satisfaction in improving business processes. I have now been a clinic supervisor for a few years and feel I have maxed my potential without learning new skills. I found coding through my sister in law who is a developer. What I found is a world of interesting puzzles that use creativity through formulas. I was able to utilize my past love for math and science but also incoporate my creativity. I am so excited for the potential for growth and opprotunity coding can bring. I also very much wish to bring representation to the coding world by being a Colombian-American woman. I think intersetionality and representation are crucial in the growth of businesses.
